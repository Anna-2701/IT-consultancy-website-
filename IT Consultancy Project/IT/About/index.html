COMPANY HISTORY

Azad IT Consultancy, founded in 2005 by Dr. Arif Azad, began with a vision to provide cutting-edge technology solutions to businesses worldwide. Starting as a small team of software engineers in Bangalore, India, the consultancy quickly gained a reputation for its innovative approach to IT services.

In its early years, Azad IT Consultancy focused on software development for local businesses, gradually expanding its client base through quality service and client referrals. By 2010, the company had established a strong presence in the IT outsourcing sector, offering bespoke solutions in web development, mobile applications, and enterprise software.

The consultancy's commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction propelled its growth, leading to partnerships with global corporations seeking cost-effective and scalable IT solutions. By 2015, Azad IT Consultancy had diversified its service offerings to include cybersecurity consulting, cloud computing solutions, and digital transformation strategies.

Throughout its history, Azad IT Consultancy has remained agile in adapting to technological advancements and market demands. It has expanded its operations beyond India, establishing offices in the United States, Europe, and Southeast Asia to better serve its international clientele.

Today, Azad IT Consultancy continues to innovate and expand its service portfolio, focusing on emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence, blockchain, and IoT. With a team of over 500 dedicated professionals, the consultancy remains committed to driving digital transformation and delivering measurable business outcomes for its clients globally.
