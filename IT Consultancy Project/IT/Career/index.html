How to apply t the job

When applying for a job at Azad IT Consultancy, please follow these instructions:

1.Subject Line:
Use a clear and specific subject line such as "Application for [Job Title] at Azad IT Consultancy".
2.Email Body:
Include a brief introduction mentioning the position you are applying for and where you found the job listing (if applicable).
3.Attach Resume:
Attach your updated resume in PDF format. Ensure it includes relevant experience, education, and skills related to the job.
4..Cover Letter (Optional):
If required or if you wish to include one, attach a cover letter highlighting why you are interested in the position and how your skills align with the job requirements.
5.Contact Information:
Provide your contact information (phone number and email address) for further communication.
6.Closing:
Close your email professionally with a statement of eagerness to discuss your application further and thank them for considering your candidacy.

Ensure your email is concise, professional, and tailored to the specific job opportunity at Azad IT Consultancy.
